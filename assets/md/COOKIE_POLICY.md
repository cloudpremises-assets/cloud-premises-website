# Cookie Policy

*Last updated: 10 October 2025*

This Cookie Policy explains how **Cloud Premises Technologies Private Limited** ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website, software, and enterprise services, including our **SaaS products and SAP consulting services** (collectively, the "Services"). This policy applies globally to all users, including enterprise clients and individual users.

---

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you access our Services. They help us:

- Enhance security and authentication for enterprise accounts
- Maintain user sessions and preferences across our platform
- Analyze service performance and user experience
- Personalize your dashboard and interface settings

---

## 2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies in our enterprise environment:

### a. Essential & Security Cookies

Critical for platform security, user authentication, and core functionality. These include session management, secure login, and access control for our SaaS products and cannot be disabled without affecting service availability.

### b. Performance & Analytics Cookies

Monitor platform performance, track system usage patterns, and identify areas for improvement in our SaaS products and SAP services. Examples include load balancing, error tracking, and service optimization.

### c. Functional & Preference Cookies

Remember your enterprise settings, dashboard configurations, API preferences, and user interface choices across our product suite.

### d. Service Optimization Cookies

Used to maintain service quality, monitor system health, and ensure optimal performance for all clients using our cloud infrastructure and SAP solutions.

---

## 3. Managing Cookies in Enterprise Environment

You can manage cookies through your browser settings, though we recommend consulting with your IT department for enterprise environments:

- Configure browser privacy settings
- Remove stored cookies from your device
- Set browser to notify before accepting cookies

⚠️ **Important**: Disabling essential cookies may prevent access to critical platform features, security protocols, and service functionality required for normal business operations.
